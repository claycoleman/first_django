Learn the ins and outs of your country

Django States is a site tailored to help you get your facts straight. Many recent studies have shown that most Americans struggle to name even 30 of the 50 states, let alone find their places on a map. I designed this site to help you step up your geography game. No one wants to be that guy who forgot that Maine was a state.

As an added perk, you've got an entire database of cities in the United States. You'll find a beautiful version of Google Maps rendering the location of each of those cities. So, that city whose name you've always heard but never knew exactly where it is can be seen on a map just by searching and navigating to the details of that city.

Also, each state page also features a map of the state with county boundaries drawn on -- besides a picture of the state's flag and Great Seal. I'm excited for you to be here! Get started by hitting one of the links below.
